# Chrome Web Store Listing Data

Use the following information when publishing Video Flow to the Chrome Web Store.

## 1. Store Metadata

* **Extension Name:** Video Flow - Premium Playback Enhancement
* **Summary (132 chars max):** Enhance your video experience with a customizable progress bar and a sleek, circular playback indicator.
* **Single Purpose Statement:** Enhances the visual progress and playback tracking of HTML5 videos with a customizable UI overlay.
* **Detailed Description:**
    Elevate your video playback experience with Video Flow.

    Video Flow is designed for users who value both aesthetics and functionality. Most web video players offer thin, hard-to-see progress bars. Video Flow fixes this by injecting a premium, customizable UI overlay that keeps you informed of your playback status at a glance.

    **Key Features:**
    ✅ **Customizable Bar:** Adjust thickness (2px - 12px) and pick any color to match your mood or setup.
    ✅ **Circular HUD:** A modern circular indicator with real-time percentage display, perfect for full-screen viewing.
    ✅ **Premium Aesthetics:** Beautiful glassmorphic backgrounds and smooth neon gradients.
    ✅ **Zero Performance Impact:** Built with optimized CSS and minimal JS to ensure your videos never lag.
    ✅ **Universal Compatibility:** Works seamlessly across major video platforms and custom HTML5 players.

    **How to use:**
    1. Install the extension.
    2. Open any video.
    3. Click the Video Flow icon in your toolbar to customize the look.

    Transform your media consumption today with Video Flow!

## 2. Privacy Policy

*   **Single Purpose:** Video Flow serves a single purpose: enhancing the visual progress indicator of HTML5 videos.
*   **Data Usage:** 
    - The extension does NOT collect, store, or transmit any user data.
    - Personal information is NOT accessed.
    - All user settings (color, height) are stored locally on your browser via Chrome's secure storage API.

## 3. Permission Justification
*   **storage:** Required to save and persist your custom settings (color, height, etc.) across browser sessions.
*   **Host Permissions (<all_urls>, file:///*):** Required to detect and inject the custom progress bar UI into HTML5 video elements across different websites and local video files.
